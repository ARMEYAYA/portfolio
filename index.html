School projects

UrbanWear

UrbanWear PH is an online shopping website that makes it easy for busy people to buy fashionable clothes from home. It aims to provide a user-friendly experience, a wide variety of stylish clothing, and helpful customer service while saving time and reducing costs.

Quezon City University Student Portal

The Quezon City University Student Online Portal is a web-based system designed to help students easily access important school information and services. It provides features such as viewing programs, subjects, grades, announcements, account summaries, and student profiles, while also allowing users to explore campus information like organizations, mission and vision, and the history of the university.

Program Adventure

This project is a gamified coding learning platform designed to make programming more fun and engaging for students. It allows players to learn coding concepts through lessons, challenges, and a 1v1 battle mode where correct answers allow players to attack their opponent. The game also provides instant feedback, rewards, and level progression to help students track their learning while enjoying the experience.
